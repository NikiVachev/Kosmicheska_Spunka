MOMTS INCIDENT REPORT

DOCUMENT ON MISSING PERSONS

Internal circulation only
Document ID MOMTS-INC-3062-05-29
Classification Level Ω — Existential Threat

General Information

Date
May 29, 3062
Location
Central building of the Main Organization for Multiversal Travel Through Space (MOMTS)
Status
Active incident / zone of unknown risk

Event Summary

On May 29, 3062, during what appeared to be a completely normal working day, all employees and visitors inside the MOMTS building disappeared simultaneously and without a trace. Shortly before the disappearance, mass alarm systems were activated and enormous energy spikes were recorded, exceeding all known safe and measurable limits.

Observed Facts

  • Information from all surveillance cameras is completely inaccessible; recordings are missing, corrupted, or classified for unknown reasons.
  • Personal belongings (clothing, devices, documents) were found exactly where their owners were last seen.
  • All internal systems continued operating normally after the incident.
  • No signs of evacuation, struggle, or external forced entry were detected.

Subsequent Investigation Attempts

  • Initial rescue and investigation teams sent into the building never returned.
  • Autonomous drones lose signal seconds after crossing the main entrance.
  • Robotic probes cease all data transmission immediately upon entry.

Authorities’ Response

All planetary and interplanetary authorities have declared the area strictly forbidden. The building is under permanent quarantine with no physical personnel presence allowed.

Hypotheses (Unconfirmed)

  1. An uncontrolled multiversal rift.
  2. Automatic activation of a mass displacement experiment.
  3. A closed spatial loop with no exit.
  4. An intentional event involving an unknown intelligence.

Current Status

  • Number of missing persons: Unknown (estimated between 3,000 and 8,000)
  • Access: Absolutely prohibited
  • Classification: Level Ω — Existential Threat
  • Expected Development: The ITPG (Interstellar Threat Prevention Group) from the planet Varna is expected to issue an official response detailing the measures it will adopt to contain, study, or neutralize the incident.

Conclusion

The incident of May 29, 3062 remains one of the most dangerous and unexplained events in recorded human history. Any attempt to enter the building results in immediate disappearance, while the underlying cause remains unknown.

Note: This document is subject to continuous revision as new data becomes available.
